Ethan McCarthy

Hello! My name is Ethan McCarthy.

I am a mathematician and programmer located in Florida, working in the fields of computability theory and theoretical computer science.

I graduated with a Ph.D. in Mathematics from the University of Wisconsin—Madison in 2018, under the direction of Joe Miller.

Most recently, I have been employed by the Department of Mathematics at the University of Florida, working with the research group in Mathematical Logic.
